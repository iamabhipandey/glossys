Cancellation & Returns

Our refund and returns policy lasts 30 days. If 30 days have passed since your purchase, we can’t offer you a full refund or exchange.

To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging.

Refunds or Cancellations

Bellaluna hopes that you never have to cancel or return a product. At the same time, we are here to help you whenever you do so due to certain circumstances. As a first step, we are laying out our cancellation and return policy to take care of your hassles.

Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you that we have received your returned item. We will also notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

If you are approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your credit card or original method of payment within 5-7 business days.

Order has not been shipped yet:

Any order which has not been shipped can be cancelled partially or fully. The amount paid by you (if it is a prepaid order) will be refunded and should reflect in your account in 5-7 days.

Order was damaged when received:

We take utmost care while packaging to ensure that the product reaches you in perfect condition. However, due to situations beyond our control, items delivered might get damaged in transit. We will be happy to replace or cancel your order in such cases. Please inform us of the damage on the same day you receive it.

Incorrect items received:

We pride ourselves on the robustness of our fulfillment process to ensure the right shipment reaches the right person at the right place. In case you receive items that are different from what you ordered, we will arrange to have the incorrect items picked up from your address and will ship the correct items to you at the earliest.

Any other reasons for cancellation or return will be handled on a case-by-case basis. Please also refer to our Terms & Conditions for the complete cancellation policy.

Need help?
