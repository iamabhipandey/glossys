MEDICAL DEVICE STANDARDS

Loche Bio designs and supplies modular cleanroom environments specifically engineered for medical device, cosmetic, and pharmaceutical production. Our cleanrooms are built to meet strict regulatory expectations — including ISO 14644 standards and GMP guidelines — while remaining flexible enough for rapid deployment.

Medical device production requires tight control over airborne particulates, pressure differentials, and humidity. Loche Bio’s systems are tested and validated to ensure Class 100 (ISO 5) and other target classifications as required by your process.

  • Custom modular layouts: free-standing or wall-integrated solutions.
  • Precision environmental control: pressure, temperature, humidity, and contamination monitoring.
  • Compliance-ready: engineered to support ISO 14644 and ISO 13485 requirements.
Recommended: For medical device packaging and sterile assembly, choose Hardwall Cleanrooms to achieve industry-standard control and repeatability.

ADDITIONAL INSTALLATIONS

Loche Bio supplies a full suite of ancillary systems to ensure the cleanroom performs reliably and safely:

  • Air filtration: HEPA / ULPA filtration stages with pre-filter cascades.
  • Laminar flow: configurable for bench-top or full-room applications.
  • Lighting & utilities: flow-through, anti-glare LED systems optimized for production tasks.
  • Monitoring & alarms: real-time environmental telemetry and alerting.

All installations are integrated and tested with your existing HVAC and process equipment, with commissioning documentation and IQ/OQ/PQ support available.
